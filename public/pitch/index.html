1The idea, in plain words

People love scrolling properties and imagining the life inside them.

Today they scroll, fall in love… and then hit a wall: “but what is it actually worth, and could I afford it?” Every property site shows pictures. Almost none let you understand and play with the price.

🏠

They browse & dream

Beautiful listings, area by area across Australia — built for the joy of looking.

📈

They see a price

An honest, area-based estimate appears instantly — turning a daydream into a real conversation.

🎚️

They shape it

A few simple sliders let them adjust the estimate to their own assumptions. They own the number.

That’s the whole MVP. Useful on day one. And every scroll, click and adjustment quietly teaches the system what each person really wants — which is the foundation for everything that comes next.

2Why this, why now, why BrickFloor

The opportunity is hiding in plain sight.

Australia is one of the most property-obsessed nations on earth. Listing sites get enormous attention — but they stop at the photos. The single question every buyer is really asking, “what’s it worth to me?”, is left unanswered.

BrickFloor holds the one thing that’s genuinely hard to get: real, structured transaction and offer data. That’s the moat. A price estimate is only as good as the data behind it — and BrickFloor already has the data.

The flywheel

More browsing → more signals about what people want → smarter estimates → more browsing.

It’s a product that gets better the more it’s used — and the data advantage compounds over time. That is exactly the kind of story that excites later investors.

4The MVP — “The Price Explorer”

Four simple pieces. One delightful experience.

Here is exactly what €15,000 builds. Each piece is useful on its own — together they’re the thing Dean described: a place people enjoy scrolling, where the price comes alive.

Built in this MVP

a · The Area Price Engine (what the industry calls an “AVM”)

In plain terms: a smart calculator that estimates what homes are likely worth, suburb by suburb across Australia, using real market data points. It gives an honest range — not a falsely precise single number — because that’s both more truthful and more useful.

We build this to complement the price work BrickFloor is already doing — and to make it something a buyer actually wants to interact with.

Built in this MVP

b · Scroll & Dream

A clean, photo-first way to browse properties area by area. Every listing carries its estimate, so curiosity instantly becomes a real, grounded conversation about value.

This is the part people will open “just to look” — and keep coming back to.

Built in this MVP · try it →

Foundations laid in MVP · grows next

d · It quietly learns what each person loves

As someone scrolls, lingers, clicks and adjusts, the Explorer gently learns their taste — the areas, styles and price points that make them stop. In the MVP this simply means showing each person more of what they love. It’s the seed of something much bigger (see the horizon below) — but on day one it’s just a better, more personal experience.
